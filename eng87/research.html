Research in Engineering

Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory

    Director of Mechatronics and Controls Laboratory: Dr. Tsu-Chin Tsao

  • Focuses research on servos and precision control for applications in medical devices, nano-technology and manufacturing.
    • Airborne Manipulator with Twisting and Tilting Rotors for Omnidirectional Thrust Vectoring
    • Intraocular Robotic Interventional and Surgical System
    • Fast Tool Servos for Precision Machining of Irregulary Shaped Surfaces
    • Telesurgical/Telementoring Laparoscopic Surgical Robot (Laparobot)

Bionics Lab

    Director of Bionics Lab: Dr. Jacob Rosen

  • Focuses on developing wearable bio-technology to aid in recovery or motor function of the body and medical robotics for surgical and medicinal applications.
    • Raven IV - Colaborative Surgery
    • Exoskeleton Prototype 4 (EXO-UL8)
    • Trauma Pod - Phase 1: The Operating Room of the Future
    • Red Dragon (Edge)

Robotics & Mechanisms Laboratory

    Director of RoMeLa: Dr. Dennis Hong

  • Build, wire, and program robots to compete in international competitions and participate in National Challenges.
    • DARwIn's Brain-A.I. for a Soccer Playing Humanoid Robot
    • Development of an Autonomous Vehicle for the DARPA Urban Challenge
    • CHARLI: Cognitive Humanoid Autonomous Robot with Learning Intelligence
    • HyDRAS-Ascent: Design and Analysis for a Pole Climbing Serpentine Robot

Modeling of Complex Thermal Systems Laboratory

    Director of Modeling of Complex Thermal Systems Laboratory: Dr. Adrienne Lavine

  • Use simulation software, such as MatLab, to predict and optimize the thermal flow of complex systems.
    • 5 kW Closed Loop Pilot System Capable of Producing Super-Critical Steam for the Purpose of Power Generation
    • Thermodynamic and Cost Modeling of Solar Thermal Energy Storage Systems using Supercritical Fluids
    • A Barium-Titanate/Epoxy Resin Nanocomposite for Enhanced Dielectric Properties
    • Magnetic Thermomechanical Devices Used for Waste Heat Energy Harvesting and Thermal Conductance Switching
