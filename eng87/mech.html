Mechanical Engineering

Description

Mechanical engineers fall under one of the broadest engineering disciplines and are required to:

  • Analyze problems to see how mechanical and thermal devices might help solve a particular problem
  • Design or redesign mechanical and thermal devices or subsystems, using analysis and computer-aided design
  • Investigate equipment failures or difficulties to diagnose faulty operation and to recommend remedies
  • Develop and test prototypes of devices they design
  • Analyze the test results and change the design or system as needed
  • Oversee the manufacturing process for the device

Branches of Mechanical Engineering

  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Acoustical Engineering
  • Manufacturing Engineering
  • Thermal Engineering
  • Production Engineering
  • Vehicle Engineering (Automotive, Naval Architecture)
