Why Did You Choose Engineering?

Angel Coronado

I chose engineering because I want the chance to challenge myself and improve the world around me. What motivates me is the hope that one day I can leave a positive impact on the world around me in some way. Mainly, I want to innovate flight technology to make air travel faster and more accessible in the future. This desire pushed me to Aerospace Engineering which allows me to observe and innovate the way objects move through and interact with fluids.
