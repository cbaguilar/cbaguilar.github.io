Skills for Succeeding as a Mechanical Engineer

In order to be able to receive a degree in Mechanical Engineering, students must be able to:

  • Have strong critical thinking skills
  • Have good communication skills
  • Work coherently with others in group settings
  • Have strong technical skills
  • Be able to think “outside of the box”

These skills are what allow good students to transition well into good workers/researchers in the field of Mechanical Engineering.
