Dereferencing

2025-05-15

One of my common petty sources of annoyance in grade school was when people would see something I did and falsely assume it was a reference to some piece of mass media. I did not grow up with access to cable and did not watch very many movies with my parents, and I was pretty proud to say that many of my personal decisions came out of creativity or direct contact with friends.

Sometime around 2010 my dad bought me a fake beard and a helmet with plastic hollow horns on it from Spirit Halloween. These two accessories, when combined with some natural fiber shirts and a sheepskin rug that one of my Mexican uncles gave us, facilitated a very fun and easy to wear viking costume that I wore for several years. I loved wearing that costume, but it alwasy bothered me that other kids would assume that I was trying to be a character from Dreamworks' How to Train your Dragon.

The winter across 2017 and '18 was unusually wet and rainy in Southern California.
